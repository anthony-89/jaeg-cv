PhD candidate in Experimental Cardiology (Cardiovascular Science) with extensive experience in Clinical Medicine, Biomedical Sciences, and Translational Cardiovascular Research. My work in Dr. Peter van der Meer's lab has provided me with a solid foundation in both clinical and molecular data analysis, including AI-driven analytics. I am eager to apply my expertise to advance vital clinical research programs and am willing to relocate within Europe.
